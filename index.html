About Us

Prevenza Foundation is a non-profit organization aimed at promoting overall wellness
and preventing illness through medical screening services, health education, first aid, and Basic Life Support training.
With a team of experienced healthcare practitioners,
we are committed to delivering high-quality and
personalized preventive care to targeted groups who need these services.

Mission

To be the foremost advocate for preventive healthcare practices, inspiring a healthier and well-informed society.

Vision

Empowering individuals and communities with proactive healthcare knowledge through comprehensive and accessible preventive healthcare services, education, and training.
